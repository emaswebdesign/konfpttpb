Ewa Pragłowska

Ewa Pragłowska, dr n. hum., specialist in clinical psychology. Cognitive-behavioural psychotherapist and supervisor, PTTPB, EABCT. Cooperation and Teaching Director of the Cognitive-Behavioural Clinic at SWPS University of Social Sciences and Humanities, where she co-directs the Cognitive-Behavioural Psychotherapy School. Author of numerous research articles, books, and chapters on affective and personality disorders, PTSD, and cognitive-behavioural psychotherapy.
