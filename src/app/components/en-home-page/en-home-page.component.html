Welcome

We have the honor and pleasure to invite you to the International Scientific Conference: “Cognitive-Behavioual Therapy.Beyond Individual Psychotherapy” of the Polish Association of Behavioural and Cognitive Therapy, which takes place on May 8-10, 2020 in Krakow. The aim of the conference is to present contemporary reports from research and professional practice of cognitive-behavioral psychotherapy in a wide range of therapeutic interventions: individual, group, family and couple therapy. The approaches to cognitive-behavioral therapy in such a wide spectrum of interventions depend on therapists professional interests, those which are the most common in Poland it are automatically identified as individual contacts. Outstanding speakers from Poland and abroad during their workshops and plenary lectures will bring participants closer to theory and applications of cognitive-behavioral psychotherapy at a global level. Those speeches of Polish researchers and practitioners are a great opportunity to share knowledge on the progress and development of cognitive-behavioral therapies in our country.

We hope that the scientific tradition and atmosphere of Krakow as well as its unique, historical architecture are conducive to the exchange of knowledge and experience.

Details about the conference and registration will appear soon on the website.
