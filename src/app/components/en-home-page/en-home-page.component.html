Welcome

We are pleased to once again invite you to participate in the “Cognitive-Behavioral Psychotherapy. Beyond Individual Psychotherapy” international scientific conference, hosted by the prof. Zdzisław Bizoń Polish Association of Cognitive and Behavioral Therapy. The conference will take place online on April 2-4 2021. Though we were hoping to meet you in person, the current circumstances have forced us to move online.

During the conference, you will have opportunities to learn about the latest findings from cognitive-behavioral psychotherapy research and practice in many areas of application: individual, group, as well as family and couples’ therapy. Such a wide perspective is particularly innovative considering that in Poland, cognitive-behavioral psychotherapy is usually associated with individual therapy. During their workshops and plenary lectures, expert guests both from Poland and abroad will present the theory and practice of cognitive-behavioral psychotherapy on a world-class level. Presentations by Polish researchers and practitioners will serve as an opportunity to share knowledge on the growth and development of cognitive-behavioral psychoterapy in our country.

The conference will include lectures, workshops, supervision sessions, symposia, and poster sessions. The conference program has remained largely unchanged. It will soon be published on the conference website.

Participation in the conference will be counted towards teaching hours (godziny dydaktyczne) required for renewing the PTTPB Psychotherapist and Supervisor-Teacher Certificate.

See you soon,
