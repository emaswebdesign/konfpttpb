Transport

Despite the fact that Krakow is a huge agglomeration, getting around seems to be quite easy. The city is famous for its efficient and well-developed public transportation. In Krakow, you can move freely both by tram and by bus. An additional attraction for residents and tourists is the possibility of renting an ecological bicycle or scooter. You can also get around Krakow by car or taxi. Remember if you decide to travel by car you must check which roads are available for cars and that you must pay parking fee in this area.

The network of taxi services and short-term car rental (Radiotaxi, Mytaxi, Uber, Panek, Traficar) is also developed in Krakow.
