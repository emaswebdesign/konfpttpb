Wiesław Jerzy Cubała

Prof. dr hab. n. med. Wiesław Jerzy Cubała works at the Adult Psychiatry Clinic of the Medical University of Gdańsk, which he has been affiliated with from the beginning of his career. His interests include mood and anxiety disorders in particular, to which he has devoted his research and clinical work. He has authored and co-authored numerous publications in this area, including his participation in formulating the standards of pharmacological treatment of select mood-anxiety disorders as well as in preparing a monograph on this diagnostic category. He is also a member of the World Federation of Societies of Biological Psychiatry (WFSBP) Task Force on Mental Disorders in Primary Care, and is actively publishing expert texts on the diagnosis and treatment of anxiety disorders in this forum.
