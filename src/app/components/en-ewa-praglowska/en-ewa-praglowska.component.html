Ewa Pragłowska

University of Social Sciences and Humanities, ACSTEC Advanced Clinical Studies and Therapy Excellence Center

PhD. specialist in clinical psychology, psychotherapist and supervisor of cognitive-behavioral psychotherapy. PTTPB, SITCC, EABCT, Polish Psychiatric Association and supervisor certificates. Member of Członek EACLIPT (European Association of Clinical Psychology and Psychological Treatment). Until 2006, she worked at the Department of Affective Diseases of the Institute of Psychiatry and Neurology in Warsaw. In the years 2008-2018, a clinical co-director, supervisor and psychotherapist in TRAKT I-III research programs regarding min. the effectiveness of prolonged exposure therapy in patients with PTSD. Co-director of the postgraduate School of Cognitive Behavioral Psychotherapy at the University of Social Sciences and Humanities in Warsaw, Sopot, Wrocław, Katowice, Poznań and Rzeszów and studies in the supervision of cognitive-behavioral therapy. Co-author of the first Polish textbooks on cognitive-behavioral therapy, supervision and prevention in cognitive-behavioral psychotherapy.
