Anita Bryńska

Dr. hab. n. med. Anita Bryńska - doctor, works in the Developmental Psychiatry Clinic of the Medical University of Warsaw and Centrum CBT, specialist in child and adolescent psychiatry, certified therapist and supervisor-teacher of the Polish Association for Behavioural and Cognitive Therapy. Her interests include the diagnosis and treatment of developmental psychiatric disorders, in particular obsessive-compulsive disorder, anxiety disorders, and other emotional (neurotic) disorders, mood disorders, tic disorders, attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD), and autism spectrum disorders, including Asperger’s syndrome. Cognitive-behavioural therapist and supervisor.
